1.1 Endpoint Detection & Response (EDR)

“In today’s threat landscape, traditional antivirus is no longer enough. Endpoint Detection & Response (EDR) delivers advanced, real-time visibility and protection for every device in your organization — laptops, desktops, and servers — whether on-site or remote.

EDR goes beyond prevention, enabling your security teams to detect, investigate, and neutralize threats before they escalate. With AI-driven analytics, automated response actions, and deep forensic insights, EDR solutions help you stay ahead of sophisticated attacks like ransomware, fileless malware, and advanced persistent threats (APTs).

At Strynova, we partner with industry-leading EDR vendors — including CrowdStrike Falcon, Sophos Intercept X, and SentinelOne Singularity — to provide solutions that combine speed, accuracy, and intelligent automation. The result: stronger security, faster incident response, and minimized business disruption.”
